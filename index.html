About Us

Have you ever heard about e-Sports?

The e-Sports are online multiplayer games that are played all around the world, like: League of Legends, World of Warcraft, StarCraft. This games are watched and played by a lot of people each year. There even exist professional teams that participate in massive events in many countries. A Federation is an entity that regulates something such as FIFA or NBA but in our case, we do not work with traditional like football or basketball. FENADE as a federation, regulates and organizes the e-Sports in Costa Rica. It also promotes them in the gamers community being the only official entity in this innovative market, with lots of possibilities that were not available before.

Our project

FENADE is the national federation of electronic sports. Our objective is to promote the e-Sports as a new and fresh entertainment media inside our country by professional gaming teams and by hosting special events and gaming conventions with special thematic.

Our mainly value is teamwork, because the e-Sports are multiplayer games, where you need to cooperate with your partners to reach a common goal.

  • Importance

    Inside our country there was not an official federation for electronics sports, by meaning there is not an institution that could represent the big community of gamers and gaming teams on Costa Rica by international way. The participation of Costa Rica on gaming media is very limited, therefore FENADE represents an opportunity for this community to stand out. Nowadays the Costa Rican community have a negative perspective about videogames, people think they are a distraction for youngers generation, and it is for this reason that our mainly challenge is to deal with the actual prejudices about video games.

  • Steps

    To position FENADE as a recognized company first we needed to define what we want, who we are, and who our target is. To be known by our target and get their attention, we had to take actions and created striking advertising, mainly in social media like Facebook, for example; and based on this information we created the strategy, this means that we established FENADE as the only Costa Rican federation for e-Sports. And finally, we made our advertising campaign to motivate them to join us.

  • Innovative aspects

    In Costa Rica, many people play e-Sports, but most of them do not play as professionals because they do not have opportunities, some of them have their own teams, but they are not popular outside or inside the country, because they do not receive enough support, and that is what we want to change with our company FENADE. And what else is innovative about our project inside the country? The way people work with us, because we organized gaming tournaments in our events with different disciplines working with official national teams. Gamers all around the country want to become famous and get money by playing videogames, so we, as the national federation of electronic sports gives them the tools to make their dreams come true. Other things that make us different are the seriousness and services that we offer, for example: we have personal coaches for each team, flexible task schedules that gamers associated can complete weekly, and we have good facilities as our own offices and training rooms for the teams. In this way, gamers will not neglect their responsibilities on their homes with their families, their studies or their jobs.
